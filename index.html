ABOUT ME

Hi! I am an aspiring Web Developer based in Orange County. I first delve into basic HTML and CSS when I was in middle school. Just being a teenager trying to impress her classmates by having the best XANGA webpage. At that time I did not know that being a web developer was a career option.

I went to school at Cal State Long Beach to get a Bachelors in Business Administration. However, during school I worked at a bakery where I was able to use my imagination/creativity and transform it into reality thru cake decorating. From sculpting 3d figurines with fondant and piping buttercream my imagination was able to run wild.

After graduation I worked at a couple medical offices: in radiology, pediatric and finally at a nursing care center. I was doing medical billing and book keeping. However, I was finding myself unfulfilled and wanting to be more creative. My brother in law who is a software engineer introduced me to the world of web developing. Thru a leap of faith I decided to change my career path October 2021 and pursue a career in web development. I hope that thru my lines of codes I could use my creativity to create applications that other people can enjoy as much as I do. This is only the beginning, but please follow me as I hone and learn more skills.
